Development Best Practices
1. Understand requirements and design
2. Develop the queries based on the design
3. Unit Testing and Functionality Testing: 
    Evaluate result against the requirements
    (identify bugs if any)
4. Troubleshoot the issues
    a. Run queries on underlying tables
    b. Make sure the conditions have relevant logic or 
        right logic
    c. Check for right join conditions
5. Come up with final solution
